Bolognese

Description

Recipe passed on from my Italian grandfather. The ultimate comfort food. Leftovers freeze nicely! To make this recipe more authentic, you can use a mixture of ground veal, pork, and beef. You can also substitute the balsamic vinegar for 1/2 cup of red wine.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Onion and garlic
  • Ground beef
  • Herbs
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti.
  2. Sautee onion and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add ground beef and brown off.
  4. Add tinned tomatoes and stir thoroughly.
  5. Add herbs.
  6. Drain spaghetti and put in bowl.
  7. Top spaghetti with meat sauce and cheese.
