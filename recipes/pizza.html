Pizza

Description

This is a fantastic version of an Italian classic. The feta cheese adds a rich flavor that brings this dish to life. Incredibly easy and incredibly delicious!

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion and garlic
  • Sea salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Cook tomatoes with onion and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Form pizza dough into pizza shape.
  4. Top pizza dough with tomato sauce.
  5. Slice mozzarella and arrange on top of pizza.
  6. Top with basil leaves.
  7. Bake for 10 minutes until cheese is golden brown.
